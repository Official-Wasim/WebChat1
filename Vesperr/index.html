About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this chat app?

This chat app is designed to facilitate real-time communication and collaboration among users on our website. It allows users to engage in text-based conversations, share information, and connect instantly.

How do I start using the chat app?

To start using the chat app, simply navigate to the get started section of our website. Sign up and log in. Click on a conversation to open the chat window and begin messaging.

Can I use the chat app on mobile devices?

Yes, our chat app is responsive and can be accessed on various devices, including smartphones and tablets. You can use the browser on your mobile device to access the chat without needing to download any additional apps.

Is my conversation history saved?

Yes, your conversation history is saved within the app. You can scroll back to view previous messages and refer to important information shared in past conversations.

Can I create group chats?

Yes, you can create group chats by adding multiple contacts to a conversation. Group chats are a great way to collaborate with teams or coordinate with friends and family.
